Mince Pies

45 minutes
easy
added by Dale-Anthony

Ingredients

500g shop-bought mincemeat
175g chopped walnuts
2 apples - peeled, cored and chopped
100g dark brown soft sugarv 4 tablespoons dark rum
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Method / Instruction

Combine mincemeat, walnuts, apples, brown sugar, lemon juice and rum in a bowl. Mix well. Cover and refrigerate overnight.

Preheat oven to 220 C / Gas mark 7.

Take filling out of refrigerator and let come to room temperature. Prepare pastry and cut out circles to line a patty tin. Stir filling well and pour into base. Top with pastry and make slits or make a star-shaped top. Crimp edges.

Bake in preheated oven on low shelf for 40 minutes or until golden brown.
